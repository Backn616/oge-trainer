Task 4 — Variant 1

Imagine that you and your friend are doing a school project “Spending holidays: in the city or in the countryside”. You have found some illustrations and want to share the news. Leave a voice message to your friend. In 2.5 minutes be ready to:

  • explain the choice of the illustrations for the project by briefly describing them and noting the differences;
  • mention the advantages (1–2) of spending holidays in the city and in the countryside;
  • mention the disadvantages (1–2) of each type of holiday;
  • express your opinion on the subject of the project – which way of spending holidays presented in the pictures you prefer and why.

You will speak for not more than 3 minutes (12–15 sentences). You have to talk continuously.
