Task 1. Imagine that you are preparing a project with your friend. You have found some interesting material for the presentation and you want to read this text to your friend. You have 1.5 minutes to read the text silently, then be ready to read it out loud. You will not have more than 1.5 minutes to read it.

Sherlock Holmes is a famous fictional character from British literature. He was created by Sir Arthur Conan Doyle. Holmes is a private detective who lives in London. Many people know him because of his great mind and amazing logical thinking. He solves difficult crimes that others cannot. In the stories, Holmes is usually described as tall and thin. He often wears a hat and smokes a pipe. He is very intelligent and notices the smallest details. He doesn’t talk much and prefers to work alone or with his friend Dr Watson. Holmes likes music and sometimes plays the violin. Although Holmes is not a real person, he has become a symbol of clever thinking and observation. People all over the world read stories about him and watch films and series. Many believe that Sherlock Holmes is the best detective character ever created.
