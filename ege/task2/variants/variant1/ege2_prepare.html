The Happy Frying Pan Culinary School: Learn to cook with us!

You are considering applying to the Happy Frying Pan Culinary School and now you’d like to get more information. In 1.5 minutes you are to ask four direct questions to find out about the following:
  1. tuition fee;
  2. application deadline;
  3. length of the course;
